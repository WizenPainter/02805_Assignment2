==Guardians of the Galaxy== =\nIn an alternate future, Wonder Man - now with snow white hair and using the alias \"Hollywood\" - reluctantly aids the [[Guardians of the Galaxy (1969 team)|Guardians of the Galaxy]]. He also aids several other heroes, sometimes operating out of the still-standing [[Avengers Mansion]]. Hollywood eventually joins the Guardians,<ref>''Guardians of the Galaxy'' #62 (July 1995). Marvel Comics.</ref> and later the \"breakaway\" team, the [[Galactic Guardians]].<ref>''Galactic Guardians'' #1-4 (July \u2013 November 1994). Marvel Comics.</ref>\n\nIn the 2008 ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy (2008 team)|Guardians of the Galaxy]]'' series, he is shown as part of the Guardians in a potential 3009 AD. Here he fought under the direction of [[Killraven]] and was later allied with a \"modern\" version of the Guardians.<ref>''Guardians of the Galaxy'' vol. 2 #18 (November 2009). Marvel Comics.</ref>\n
