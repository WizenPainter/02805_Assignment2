==Victorius== =\n{{For|the [[cyborg]] son of [[Ultron]] who also goes by \"Victorious\"|Victor Mancha}}\n{{Infobox comics character| <!--Wikipedia:WikiProject Comics--> \nimage=\n|caption=\n|character_name=Victorius\n|real_name=Victor Conrad\n|species=\n|publisher=[[Marvel Comics]]\n|debut=''[[Astonishing Tales]]'' #18 (June 1973)\n|creators=[[Mike Friedrich]] and [[Dan Adkins]]\n|alliances=[[Advanced Idea Mechanics|A.I.M.]]<br/>Cult of Entropy\n|aliases=Scientist Supreme\n|powers=Peak physical condition, assorted combat training\n}} \n \n'''Victor Conrad''' was the second individual to use the Scientist Supreme persona within A.I.M.. An A.I.M. scientist working for [[Advanced Idea Mechanics|A.I.M.]] who succeeded in duplicating the Super-Soldier Serum which had transformed Steve Rogers into [[Captain America]], Conrad drank the serum himself and became a physically perfect human being. Naming himself '''Victorius''', he first attempted to take over A.I.M.{{issue|date=December 2011}} Failing to do so, he later adopted the death-worshiping philosophy of the Cult of Entropy and made himself their new leader. He stole the [[Cosmic Cube]] to use to create Jude the Entropic Man as a means of spreading destruction.<ref>''Marvel Two-in-One'' #42''</ref> Jude rebelled against Victorius, and when Jude, Victorius, the Cube and the [[Man-Thing]] all came into physical contact with each other, Jude and Victorius were transformed into a form of radiant crystal.<ref>''Marvel Two-in-One'' #43</ref> Years later, [[Andrew Forson]] reveals that [[Mockingbird (Marvel Comics)|Mockingbird]] had been secretly working for him at the time and that Mockingbird was used to \"get Victorius out of the way\" in order to take over the Cult of Entropy.<ref>''Secret Avengers'' #14 (March 2014)</ref>\n
